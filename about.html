Our Story

Shradha Handicraft was founded with the mission to bring traditional craftsmanship to modern homes. Our artisans are masters of their craft, creating pieces that are not only beautiful but also hold cultural significance. We started as a small initiative in a local community and have grown into a brand that celebrates and supports artisans from various regions.

Our journey began in a small village where the art of handcrafting was passed down through generations. Today, we connect these talented artisans with a global audience, offering them a platform to showcase their work and earn a sustainable livelihood.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve traditional arts and crafts while empowering artisans. We believe in creating opportunities for artisans to thrive by providing them with the tools and resources they need to succeed in the modern market.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading name in the handicraft industry, known for our commitment to quality, authenticity, and social responsibility. We aim to inspire a greater appreciation for handmade crafts and to foster a deeper connection between our customers and the artisans who create these beautiful products.

Our Values

  • Quality: We are committed to providing the highest quality products that are made with care and precision.
  • Authenticity: Each product we offer is a reflection of the artisan's skill and cultural heritage.
  • Sustainability: We use sustainable materials and practices to ensure the longevity of both our products and the environment.
  • Empowerment: We empower artisans by providing fair wages and opportunities for growth and development.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate individuals dedicated to preserving the art of handmade crafts. From designers to artisans, everyone at Shradha Handicraft plays a vital role in bringing our products to life.

Our team includes skilled artisans, designers, quality control experts, and customer service professionals who work together to ensure that every product meets our high standards.

Community Impact

We believe in giving back to the community. Through various initiatives, we support local schools, healthcare facilities, and environmental projects. By choosing Shradha Handicraft, you are contributing to these efforts and helping to make a positive impact on the lives of many.

Our community programs include:

  • Educational scholarships for children of artisans.
  • Healthcare initiatives to ensure the well-being of our artisans and their families.
  • Environmental conservation projects to protect the natural resources used in our crafts.
