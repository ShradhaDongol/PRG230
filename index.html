Our Story

Shradha Handicraft was born out of a passion for preserving traditional crafts and empowering artisans. Our journey began in a small village where we saw the incredible talent of local craftsmen and women. Today, we work with artisans from various regions, providing them with a platform to showcase their skills and earn a sustainable livelihood.

We believe in the power of handmade products to connect people and cultures. Each item in our collection is a testament to the hard work, dedication, and creativity of our artisans. By choosing Shradha Handicraft, you are not only bringing home a beautiful piece of art but also supporting a community of talented individuals.
